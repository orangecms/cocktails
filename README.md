# Cocktail Recipes

A small collection of very simple :cocktail: recipes.
Usually for tall, 400ml glasses. Improvise as necessary.

## Programmer's Poison

This will make a nice, dark green, poisonous looking mixture.
And it's sweeter than honey. Stir well.

- crushed ice
- 300 ml cola
- 4cl blue curacao

## Debugger's Delusion

Delightful and exciting it starts, just like a true debug session.

- crushed ice
- 300ml mate iced tea
- 4cl absinthe

## Coder's Cocktail

Just in case you need to get really serious.

- ice cubes
- 2cl each vodka, gin, rum, tequila, herbal liqueur
- 250ml energy drink

## Bloody Aero

On a budget flight to a conference? Ask for this:

- tomato juice (usually served in a cup), salt, pepper
- vodka (they have tiny bottles)
- hope for ice

## Mind Injection

Keeps you awake until you're drunk

- crushed ice, brown sugar
- 2cl rum, 2cl tequila, 2cl lime juice
- fill up with energy (alternative volt cola, everything high caffeine)
